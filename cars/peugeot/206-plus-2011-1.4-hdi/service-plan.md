# Service plan

This is a practical private service plan. Adjust it when official service data is found.

## Baseline service now

Because the car has an unknown/old service history, do this once to reset the baseline:

| Task | Danish | English | Priority | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Engine oil | Motorolie | Engine oil | High | Use correct oil spec, fill gradually |
| Oil filter | Oliefilter | Oil filter | High | OEM 1109 AY |
| Drain plug gasket | Bundprop pakning | Oil drain plug gasket | High | OEM 0313 40, replace every oil change |
| Air filter | Luftfilter | Air filter | High | OEM 1444 TV |
| Cabin filter | Kabinefilter / pollenfilter | Cabin filter / pollen filter | High | OEM 6447 AZ; choose activated carbon if possible |
| Fuel filter | Brændstoffilter | Fuel filter cartridge | High | OEM 1906 E6 |
| Brake inspection | Bremsetjek | Brake inspection | High | Look at pad thickness, disc rust, seized calipers |
| Tyres | Dæk | Tyres | High | Check tread, cracks, age, pressure |
| Lights | Lys | Lights | High | Check all exterior lights |
| Coolant | Kølervæske | Coolant | Medium | Change if unknown/dirty/old |
| Brake fluid | Bremsevæske | Brake fluid | Medium | Change/test if older than 2 years |
| A/C test | Aircondition test | A/C diagnostics | Medium | See diagnostics/aircondition.md |
| Rust check | Rusttjek | Rust inspection | High | Especially underside, sills, suspension mounts, brake lines |

## Ongoing intervals

| Interval | Tasks |
|---|---|
| Every 12 months / 15,000 km | Engine oil, oil filter, general inspection |
| Every 24 months | Cabin filter, brake fluid test/change, coolant check |
| Every 30,000 km | Air filter, deeper inspection |
| Every 40,000-60,000 km | Fuel filter cartridge |
| Every 4-5 years | Coolant change if not long-life confirmed |

## Pre-MOT / syn checklist

- [ ] No warning lights
- [ ] Brakes even and strong
- [ ] Handbrake works
- [ ] No oil/fuel/brake fluid leaks
- [ ] Tyres legal and not cracked
- [ ] Lights and horn work
- [ ] Wipers and washer work
- [ ] Seatbelts lock and retract
- [ ] Suspension has no obvious play/noise
- [ ] Exhaust is tight and not leaking
- [ ] Brake lines not heavily corroded
- [ ] No structural rust holes
