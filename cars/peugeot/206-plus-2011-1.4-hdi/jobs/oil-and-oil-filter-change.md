# Job: Oil and oil filter change

## Parts

| Danish | English | OEM / spec |
|---|---|---|
| Motorolie | Engine oil | 5W-30 PSA B71 2290 |
| Oliefilter | Oil filter | 1109 AY |
| Bundprop pakning | Oil drain plug gasket / sump plug washer | 0313 40 |

## Tools

- Jack + axle stands or ramps
- Wheel chocks
- Oil drain pan
- Funnel
- Socket/spanner for drain plug
- Oil filter tool/socket as needed
- Gloves and rags
- Torque wrench if official torque is found

## Important notes

- Work on level ground.
- The car must be safely supported before going underneath.
- Do not overfill engine oil.
- Used oil and filter must be delivered to a recycling station.

## Short procedure

1. Warm the engine slightly, then switch off.
2. Lift/support the car safely.
3. Remove the oil filler cap and pull dipstick slightly.
4. Place drain pan under sump.
5. Remove drain plug and drain oil.
6. Replace oil filter.
7. Fit new drain plug gasket.
8. Refit drain plug.
9. Fill approx. 3.3 L oil first.
10. Wait, check dipstick, top up slowly.
11. Start engine briefly and check for leaks.
12. Wait 5 minutes and re-check oil level.
